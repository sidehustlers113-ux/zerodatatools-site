Help & Block Types

How it works

Click a block type in the left palette to add it to the canvas. Type your content, drag to reorder, toggle blocks on/off without deleting. The live preview updates instantly. Copy or download when done — your session auto-saves to localStorage.

Block types

  • Role Who the AI is and its persona
  • Context Background the AI needs
  • Task What the AI must do
  • Constraints What it must not do
  • Format How to structure output
  • Tone Voice and register
  • Examples Sample input/output pairs
  • Custom Free-form block for anything else

Output formats

MD — Markdown headers (## Role). XML — XML tags (<role>). Plain — labelled sections, no markup.

Privacy

Runs entirely in your browser. No content is ever sent to a server. No account required. No telemetry of any kind.
